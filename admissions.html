Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent with strong performance in mathematics and science
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent)
  • Completion of calculus, physics, and chemistry courses
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional for 2025 admissions)

Required Documents

  • Completed online application form
  • Official high school transcripts
  • Two letters of recommendation (preferably from STEM teachers)
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Resume or list of extracurricular activities
  • Application fee: $75 (fee waivers available)

International Students

  • TOEFL (minimum 80 iBT) or IELTS (minimum 6.5) for non-native English speakers
  • Evaluation of international transcripts
  • Proof of financial support
  • Copy of passport
