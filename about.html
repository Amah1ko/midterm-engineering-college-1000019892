About Engineering College

Excellence in engineering education since 1985

Our Mission

Engineering College is dedicated to preparing innovative engineers who will lead in solving complex global challenges. We combine rigorous academics with hands-on experience, fostering critical thinking and ethical leadership.

Our faculty consists of accomplished researchers and industry professionals who bring real-world expertise into the classroom. With modern facilities and strong industry partnerships, we provide students with opportunities to apply their knowledge through internships, co-op programs, and collaborative research projects.

Our Values

  • ✓ Innovation: Encouraging creative problem-solving and entrepreneurial thinking
  • ✓ Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in education and research
  • ✓ Integrity: Upholding ethical principles in all endeavors
  • ✓ Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with industry and community

Our Programs

Choose from five specialized engineering disciplines

Computer Engineering

Master the design and development of computer systems, software, and intelligent applications.

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)

Degree: Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering

Core Topics

  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture and Organization
  • Software Engineering Principles
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Database Systems and Cloud Computing
  • Cybersecurity and Network Design

Career Paths

Software Developer, AI Engineer, Systems Architect, Cybersecurity Analyst, Data Scientist

Electrical Engineering

Design electrical systems, power generation solutions, and electronic devices that power modern life.

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)

Degree: Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering

Core Topics

  • Circuit Analysis and Design
  • Electromagnetics and Wave Theory
  • Power Systems and Renewable Energy
  • Control Systems and Automation
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Microelectronics and VLSI Design

Career Paths

Power Systems Engineer, Electronics Designer, Control Systems Engineer, Renewable Energy Specialist, Telecommunications Engineer

Civil Engineering

Plan, design, and oversee construction of infrastructure that shapes our communities and environment.

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters) or 3 years (accelerated)

Degree: Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

Core Topics

  • Structural Analysis and Design
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Transportation Systems
  • Water Resources and Hydraulics
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Construction Management

Career Paths

Structural Engineer, Transportation Planner, Construction Manager, Environmental Engineer, Urban Infrastructure Designer

Mechanical Engineering

⚙️

Create innovative mechanical systems, from robotics to renewable energy technologies.

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters) or 3 years (accelerated)

Degree: Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering

Core Topics

  • Thermodynamics and Heat Transfer
  • Mechanics of Materials
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Machine Design and Manufacturing
  • Robotics and Mechatronics
  • Energy Systems

Career Paths

Mechanical Designer, Automotive Engineer, HVAC Engineer, Robotics Engineer, Manufacturing Engineer

Chemical Engineering

🧪

Transform raw materials into valuable products through chemical processes and sustainable solutions.

Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)

Degree: Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering

Core Topics

  • Chemical Process Design
  • Reaction Engineering
  • Process Control and Optimization
  • Thermodynamics
  • Biochemical Engineering
  • Environmental and Safety Engineering

Career Paths

Process Engineer, Pharmaceutical Engineer, Environmental Engineer, Petrochemical Engineer, Biotechnology Specialist
